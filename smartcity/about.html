About Smart City Portal

Bhavnagar Municipal Corporation – Digital Citizen Services

Our Mission

Empowering citizens through transparent, digital governance

The Bhavnagar Smart City Management System is a digital initiative by the Government of Gujarat to modernize civic services. Our platform enables citizens to report infrastructure problems, track their resolution in real time, and engage directly with city departments.

We believe in transparent, accountable governance. Every complaint is logged, assigned, and tracked — ensuring no issue goes unnoticed.
